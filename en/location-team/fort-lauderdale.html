Our Team

HandyPro of Broward County

Jose-Louis and Antonia Escalante

Owner

Jose-Louis and Antonia Escalante are more than just business owners—they are passionate community members with a deep-rooted commitment to caring for others. Originally from Argentina, they bring a unique blend of expertise and empathy to their work. Jose, with his degree in architecture, has dedicated his career to interior design for hospitals, focusing on creating spaces that balance functionality and warmth, ensuring comfort and care are always at the forefront.

Antonia, with a strong background in healthcare, spent two years working in pediatric, geriatric, and family care units, where she assisted patients and gained invaluable experience understanding their needs. Prior to this, she dedicated four years to studying healthcare, which shaped her approach to providing personalized and compassionate care.

Together, they saw an opportunity to bring their combined experiences to serve a broader community. They joined the HandyPro family to offer homeowners, businesses, and individuals with reduced capacity the same trust, compassion, and high standards they have cultivated throughout their careers.

Their mission is simple yet powerful: to transform spaces and lives with a heartfelt approach that values every client like family. They believe in listening first, ensuring every project is personalized and every client’s voice is heard.

Whether you need to enhance the safety of your home, update your business space, or seek specialized services for individuals with reduced capacity, Jose and Antonia are here to provide solutions tailored to your needs. From homes to businesses, they are dedicated to making a meaningful impact, one project at a time.

Experience the difference that genuine care and expertise can make. Contact them today to see how they can help transform your space and bring peace of mind to your home or business.
