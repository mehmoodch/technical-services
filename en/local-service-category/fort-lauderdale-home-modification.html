Broward County Home Modification

ADA-Compliant Accessibility Solutions Fit to Your Needs

If you or someone you love is struggling to live comfortably at home due to age, an illness, or a disability, solutions are available to you that will allow you to stay right where you want. Most seniors and individuals living with disabilities prefer to continue living comfortably at home rather than moving. But too often they don't know where to turn for answers on how to modify their homes for greater mobility, accessibility, and safety. HandyPro has the solutions to help you or your loved one comfortably age in place.

We are a home modification and handyman company that uses universal design principles to provide customized home renovation services for people who can no longer live in their home as it's currently designed. We also install the necessary equipment and devices designed to create a comfortable and independent living environment. Our team of Certified Craftsmen will work closely with you to fully understand your needs and challenges and then customize an ADA-compliant solution. HandyPro offers the AIM in-home assessment for seniors and those living with disabilities.

Compassionate & Customized Solutions for Independent Living

Our bonded and insured HandyPro craftsmen are experienced at every type of home or office renovation. From widening a door or installing grab bars to complete remodeling projects requiring air filtering devices or adapting HVAC systems, we have the expertise for jobs large and small.

HandyPro is dedicated to giving you or your loved one more time to make memories in your home. We are more than just a team of handymen – we are your partner in home comfort and accessibility solutions. We understand the challenges you are facing and look forward to helping you overcome them with our aging-in-place solutions.

HandyPro makes residential and commercial repairs easy. Schedule online via our TruztPro app, or via phone: (954) 519-5050.
