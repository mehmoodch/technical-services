Our Story

"We’re A Handyman Service Company Driven to be Different. HandyPro started because my wife's grandmother was ripped off by a kitchen remodeler for $2,000. She was 80 years old in Allen Park MI. We know the only way to protect others was to partner with honest hardworking Craftsmen Who Care. That's exactly what HandyPro has been doing since 1996."
Keith Paul, Co-Founder & Founders
Keith  & Debra Paul, Founders

Helping the Most Vulnerable

By the year 1996, our company, operating as HandyPro Handyman Service, began getting a lot more calls from seniors or their family members seeking help with installing wheelchair ramps and grab bars. By the year 2008, recognizing a niche market opportunity within the aging population and people living with disabilities, HandyPro increased its offerings to include stairlifts, walk-in tubs, and specialized plumbing and door fixtures to enable people with arthritis to turn on a faucet or open a door.

Our AIM® Approach to Quality Services

HandyPro takes a detailed approach to our home modification services to ensure our clients’ unique needs and budgets are always met. Our AIM® approach includes the following:

  • Assess – Our services begin with an in-home assessment, where we’ll conduct a thorough walk-through of your or your loved one’s home to determine which services you need. We will focus on three primary areas: accessibility, mobility, and safety.
  • Inform – Our team will make recommendations about how to modify your home and the equipment and products needed to do so. We’ll also discuss how these products will work and what upkeep or maintenance will be required after installation.
  • Modify – Upon your approval, we’ll get to work. For clients who need immediate solutions, we are prepared to work quickly without ever sacrificing quality. We’ll answer any questions you may have and we’ll always be sure to stick to the predetermined budget.
