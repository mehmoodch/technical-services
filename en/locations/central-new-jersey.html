See What Places to Visit in Central New Jersey

See just a few places we have to offer!

Art in Nature's Embrace: The Grounds for Sculpture Experience in Central New Jersey

Grounds for Sculpture in Central New Jersey is an enchanting experience where art and nature seamlessly intertwine across 42 beautifully landscaped acres, featuring over 270 contemporary sculptures. This unique outdoor museum and sculpture park offers a tranquil yet stimulating environment, encouraging exploration and discovery amongst its diverse array of large-scale artworks and natural vistas.

Green Haven: Discovering Sustainability and Nature at Duke Farms in Central New Jersey

Duke Farms in Central New Jersey is an opportunity to immerse oneself in a vast environmental sanctuary, known for its commitment to sustainability and conservation across 2,740 acres of varied landscapes. This former estate offers miles of walking and biking trails, diverse habitats, beautiful gardens, and educational programs, making it a haven for nature lovers and those seeking a peaceful retreat.

Shop and Delight: The Ultimate Retail Adventure at The Mills at Jersey Gardens in Central New Jersey

The Mills at Jersey Gardens in Central New Jersey offers a comprehensive shopping experience, featuring a vast array of stores ranging from high-end brands to popular retail outlets, all in an indoor, climate-controlled environment. This expansive mall, known for its tax-free shopping on clothes and shoes, also provides a variety of dining options and entertainment, making it a popular destination for both locals and tourists.
