A Partner in Keeping Your Business Running

Managing a business requires many moving parts – often both literally and figuratively. You don’t want facility maintenance to get in the way. HandyPro makes maintaining your facility simple and easy. Whether you own a small retail store or manage a large commercial property, one thing is certain: problems will arise every now and then. Whether a light goes out, a painted wall begins to peel, or an accident leaves you with a damaged floor, you need a reliable resource that you can turn to for a fast, effective solution. At HandyPro®, we are your partner in keeping your commercial property running. We offer a variety of handyman services for facilities of all kinds.

HandyPro responds to all commercial jobs in a timely manner so that we can keep your business up and running smoothly. HandyPro has the tools to extend the life of your property and reduce maintenance costs. All of our commercial work maintains the standards of the Americans with Disabilities Act.

The Importance of Commercial Maintenance Services

HandyPro is here for you when problems arise, but we are always here to help you avoid them altogether. We highly recommend regular maintenance services to help minimize issues and avoid downtime. Plumbing and electrical problems are common in properties of all kinds. Every now and then, a pipe leaks, a light stops working, or other small problems arise. By scheduling routine commercial maintenance with our handyman team, we can help catch these problems before they disrupt your business. This will save you money, as well as time and hassle, in the long run.

Some of our services include:

  • Drywall repair
  • Carpentry
  • Plumbing
  • Electrical
  • Tile repairs
  • Painting
  • Grab bar and railing installations
  • Ramp and stairlift installations
  • Universal access fixtures
  • Cabinets
  • and much more
