Farmington Hills Drywall Repair Services

Simply Say Goodbye to Holes, Dents & Cracks

Are the walls of your home showing signs of age? Over time, drywall can endure damage from kids, pets, moving furniture, or a variety of other reasons. As common as drywall damage is, it isn’t always easy for homeowners to fix themselves. DIY drywall repairs can result in uneven results and additional problems down the road. That’s why it’s important to trust these fixes to the professionals. At HandyPro, we offer drywall repair services for your home and can handle cracks, holes, dents, and more. Whether you have a major hole that needs to be covered up or simply want to repair various scrapes and dents from years of wear and tear, our drywall repair experts are ready to get to work.

HandyPro can repair various types of drywall damage, including:

  • Holes
  • Dented corners
  • Scuffs and scrapes from furniture
  • Moisture and water damage
  • Cracked or flaking areas

Damage to drywall can result from hanging pictures, doorknobs slamming into walls, leaky plumbing, and a variety of other reasons. HandyPro is ready to tackle your drywall repairs, regardless of what caused them.

Drywall Repair & Painting

If you are planning to paint your home, it’s important that your walls are in great shape before you start. That means repairing any holes, dents, or scrapes to the drywall to ensure your paint can be applied smoothly and evenly. Even minor damages can result in less-than-stellar results. Before your next painting project, give HandyPro a call so that we can prep your walls for paint. Better yet, allow our team to handle your painting project as well as the drywall repairs for the best possible results.

The Danger of Damaged Drywall

Damaged drywall can take away from the look and feel of your space – but it can also potentially cause structural damage if not fixed in a timely manner. Cracks and damp areas can affect the integrity of your home and should be addressed as quickly as possible, especially around water! Call our drywall repair team at the first sign of damage so that we can fix it before it becomes a more costly and extensive issue.
