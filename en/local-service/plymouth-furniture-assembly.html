How Our Assembly Services Work

We offer our services in predetermined blocks of time. Your craftsmen will be scheduled to complete your projects for up to two, four, or eight hours. We ask that you provide project details and optional pictures for craftsman preparation before confirmation. Our Craftsmen have all the necessary tools. The customer will also supply products or job materials. If the job requires two craftsmen due to the size, weight, and location of the finished product, additional charges may apply.

When we are finished with the furniture assembly, we will leave behind a clean work area and dispose of project debris in trash bins. Additional time may also be needed to complete projects.

A Trusted Partner for Homeowners

In addition to our furniture assembly, we offer a variety of other handyman services. Our Certified Craftsmen can assist you with painting projects, plumbing fixture installations, drywall repairs, and mounting TVs on the wall. With one call to our team, you can cross several items off your to-do list. We look forward to being your partner in making your home last a lifetime.
