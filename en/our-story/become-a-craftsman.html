Values Integrity

The core role of our Craftsmen and women is improving the lives of our clients through basic home maintenance, repair and modification for those living with mobility challenges. Our Craftsmen use their skills of drywall repair, painting, minor plumbing, and general handyman work to enrich the lives, homes and locations of our clients every day.

As our company continues to grow, we are seeking hard-working professionals to join our team. We currently have locations throughout the country and are continuing to open even more. However, as we grow, it’s important that we always stay true to who we are. There are quite a few handyman service providers and we always want to be a step above the rest

For our Certified Craftsman position, we are looking for men and women who:

  • Value a business culture built on integrity, trust, and giving back to the community
  • Like helping people stay safe in their homes
  • Want to be rewarded for knowledge, skills, and experience
  • Want to work for an established company, have a flexible schedule and benefits

Our Code of Ethics

When HandyPro was founded, owners Keith and Deb quickly realized the craftsmen in our industry needed somewhere to call home where they would be paid on time and what they were worth. The craftsmen needed a place that they could trust and be valued as the artisans they are. They needed a company with a Code of Ethics. This inspired Keith and Deb to expand and take the company national to help not only homeowners but craftsmen as well.
